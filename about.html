About the Creator

This project was created by Mandy Xiang, major in Strategic Design and Management at Parsons School of Design. With an interest in the intersections of art, culture, and urban economics, Mandy explores how creative ecosystems shape not only the aesthetic but the economic and social life of cities.

Project Overview

Art & Vitality is a data visualization project exploring the relationship between New York City’s creative ecosystems and neighborhood-level economic indicators. By mapping gallery density alongside household income, employment rates, business density, and property values, this project reveals how cultural capital weaves through urban economies — shaping not just aesthetics, but opportunity and urban identity.

Methodology

This project uses a combination of data analysis and visualization techniques to explore the relationship between art galleries and economic vitality in NYC neighborhoods. We analyzed data from various sources, including NYC Open Data, NYU Furman Center, and the Office of the New York City Comptroller.
